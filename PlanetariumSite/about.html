Our Mission

BigPlanetarium is an educational website focused on exploring the Solar System and the Milky Way in a clear and informative way. It introduces each of the planets, explaining their basic features such as size, composition, atmosphere, and position relative to the Sun, while also providing simple but meaningful scientific details. In addition, the website expands beyond the planets by presenting the Milky Way galaxy, helping users understand where our Solar System is located within a much larger cosmic environment.


The site is carefully designed to be clear, formal, and easy to study, making it suitable for students, teachers, and anyone interested in learning about space. Its structure and content aim to guide visitors step by step, allowing them to build knowledge gradually without feeling overwhelmed. By connecting the planets to the wider galaxy, the website helps visitors understand how each part fits into one larger and interconnected cosmic system, improving both comprehension and curiosity.
